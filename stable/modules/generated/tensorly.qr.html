tensorly.qr¶

qr(a)¶

Compute the qr factorization of a matrix.

Factor the matrix a as qr, where q is orthonormal and r is upper-triangular.

Parameters
atensor, shape (M, N)

Matrix to be factored.

Returns
Q, Rtensor
